Rayahna

What is theSecond Commandment?

You shall not take the name of the LORD your God in vain. This means that you should not say God's name with ill intent. You should only use his name when you are praying to make sure that his name stays holy.

Antero

Challenges in followingthis commandment

Our culture and language often challenges our faith in disobeying the second commandment, by using slang or abbreviations that use the Lord's name in vain. Sometimes we say things along the lines of “oh my God” to emphasize or make something clear, without realizing that we are taking God out of his reverence.
